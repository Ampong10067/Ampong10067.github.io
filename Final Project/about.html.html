About Klasik Sining

Klasik Sining is an online art catalogue curated by Ann Gail Miomie Ampong and Kaela Vita, celebrating artistic expression through Animations, Traditional Drawings, Digital Drawings, Paintings, Typography, Short Films, Photography, 3D Modeling, and Cinematography. This platform was built to share our collective passion for creativity and storytelling, giving viewers an immersive experience of how art transcends form and medium. Each piece in our collection reflects individuality, collaboration, and a love for timeless art.

Meet the Creators

Ann Gail Miomie Ampong

A multimedia artist who brings imagination to life through both traditional and digital artistry. Specializes in typography, 3D modeling, and photography that captures depth and mood in every detail.

Kaela Vita

A visionary creator focusing on dynamic motion and storytelling through animation and cinematography. Dedicated to transforming everyday emotions into powerful visual experiences.

Our Vision & Mission

We believe that art should not be confined by genre or platform. Klasik Sining aims to connect creativity with technology — providing a digital space where our artworks are preserved, celebrated, and experienced. Every frame, line, and tone in this collection tells a story of persistence and artistry.
