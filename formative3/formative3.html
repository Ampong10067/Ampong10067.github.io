About Me

Hello! I’m Ann Gail Miomie Ampong, a multimedia student with a deep love for photography, and visual storytelling. Photography allows me to capture moments that reflect emotion, light, and movement, the same qualities I value in multimedia art.

I believe creativity comes from seeing beauty in ordinary things. Through photography, I get to explore new perspectives and express my ideas beyond words.

Why I Love Photography

  • It lets me express emotions through light and framing.
  • It connects my design and storytelling interests.
  • It sharpens my eye for detail and visual composition.
